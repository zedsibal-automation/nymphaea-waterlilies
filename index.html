Our Story

A quiet pond in Tarlac.

Nymphaea began as a family passion for pond gardens. From our nursery in Lawy, Capas, Tarlac, we grow, divide, and replant water lilies year-round — nurturing each rhizome until it is strong enough to bloom in your own pond.

We focus on hardy day-blooming and tropical varieties selected for the Philippine climate: Supranee Pink, Inner Light, Crimson Fire, Mangkala Ubol, and more.
